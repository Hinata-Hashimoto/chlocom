ChloCom an interactive chloroplast genome comparison viewer GitHub ↗

Two chloroplast genomes are shown top and bottom; the same (homologous) genes are linked by ribbons so you can compare their positions. Hover a gene to highlight its homolog. Scroll to zoom, drag to move. Click a gene to align the other genome to its homolog and open the sequence panel. Zoom in far enough to see the DNA sequence. Shift-drag over a track to grab the sequence of that range.

Extract sequence — or Shift-drag over a genome track
Coordinates are rotated so each genome starts at its LSC (position 0). Data built from GenBank by generate_data.py. Hover a gene to highlight its homolog; click to align & open the sequence panel (with a note field). Shift-drag a track to grab a range; click empty space to clear. ChloCom on GitHub ↗
